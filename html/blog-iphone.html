IPHONE 13 Summary

iPhone 13 mobile was launched on 14th September 2021. The phone comes with a 6.10-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1170x2532 pixels at a pixel density of 460 pixels per inch (ppi). iPhone 13 is powered by a hexa-core Apple A15 Bionic processor. The iPhone 13 supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.
