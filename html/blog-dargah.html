ASPETA DARGAH

Overview

Rahmatabad is a well known place which has the dargah of hazrat syed khwaja rahmatullah nayeb-e-rasool sarkar .he lived from 1105 AH to 1195 AH [1694 AD to 1781 AD ]Aurangzeb had just put an end to the adil shahi dynasty in 1686 AD,eight years earlier thab the birth of khwaja nayeb-e-rasool sarkar .

How to reach ?

Rahmatabad or A.S.Peta or Anumasamundram peta is situated in ASPETA mandal in Nellore district, Andhra Pradesh, India. It is well connected by road to major cities like Hyderabad, Bangalore and chennai.people in hyderabad can avail direct buses up to 7 buses daily. Its around 50 kms from Nellore. VariousRTC and private bus operates are running sleeper/seater buses to this place

Special events

In Aspeta yearly Once held a Gandham(Sandhal) it is the one of the major festival for muslims. Many people reaches here from various states of india for this festival.More than "100000" people reach here for this event.It is the one of the biggest event held in AP and telengana. Various hotels are emerging to facilitate the overwhelming response form tourists all around the country.
